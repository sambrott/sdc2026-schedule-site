Summer Design Camp provides the opportunity to experience life as a design student on a university campus, offering motivated high school students a week long, immersive introduction to design thinking, design education and careers in design. Participants live in university housing and work directly in the studios of the award-winning Art + Architecture building in the heart of the University of Tennessee, Knoxville campus and visit significant architectural sites in downtown Knoxville and the surrounding areas.
